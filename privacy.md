# Privacy Policy for FocusFlow

Effective Date: January 2025

FocusFlow (“the App”) does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

## Data Collection
The App does not:
- Collect personal data
- Track users across apps or websites
- Use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking services
- Store or transmit any user-entered information

All timer settings, session names, themes, presets, and preferences remain on the user's device.

## Spotify Integration
If the user connects Spotify, the App uses Spotify’s official SDK to:
- Request playback control permission
- Play music during a focus session

The App **does not** access:
- Listening history
- Playlists
- Account details
- Personal information

Spotify authorization is handled directly by the Spotify app and is governed by Spotify’s own privacy policy.

## Notifications
Local notifications are used only for:
- Reminding the user of upcoming focus sessions
- Alerting the user when a focus session completes

No notification data is sent off-device.

## Children’s Privacy
The App does not contain content targeted at children and does not collect any data from children under 13.

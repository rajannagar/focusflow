Privacy Policy for FocusFlow

**Effective Date: January 2025**
**Last Updated: January 2025**

FocusFlow (“the App”, “we”, “our”) is designed to help users stay focused, build habits, and sync their productivity data across devices. We respect your privacy and collect only the information necessary to operate the App effectively.

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

FocusFlow collects two categories of data:

A. Account Information (Cloud-Stored)**

If you create an account or sign in using Email or Sign in with Apple, we store the following information securely using our backend provider, Supabase:

* User ID (automatically generated)
* Email address
  (Apple may provide a private relay email if you choose “Hide My Email”)
* Full name / display name (only when provided)
* Profile-related preferences, such as:

  * Display name
  * Theme settings
  * Notification preferences
  * Timer and sound settings
  * Other future profile settings you choose to sync

We **do not** store your password. Passwords are securely managed by Supabase Authentication.

B. App Usage Data (Cloud Sync – Optional)**

If you are signed in, the App may sync certain usage data to your account so it is available across your devices, including:

* Completed focus sessions
* Habit data and progress
* Stats (e.g., total focus time, streaks)
* Custom presets or configurations

This data is **never shared** with third parties and is used only to provide App functionality.

C. Local-Only Data**

Some data remains on your device unless you choose to sync:

* Timer settings
* Notifications
* Themes
* Local avatar image
* Guest mode sessions, habits, and stats

Guest users are completely anonymous.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to:

* Create and manage your account
* Sync your data across devices
* Display personalized content in the App
* Maintain and improve App functionality
* Ensure security and prevent unauthorized access

We **do not** use your data for advertising or tracking.

3. What We Do Not Collect

FocusFlow does **not**:

* Track you across apps or websites
* Use advertising SDKs
* Use analytics that identify you personally
* Access your contacts, location, or microphone
* Access your photos except when you choose an avatar image
* Sell or share your data with advertisers or data brokers

We collect only what you explicitly provide or what is needed to operate core features.

4. Third-Party Services

We use **Supabase** for secure authentication and cloud data storage.
Supabase provides:

* User authentication
* Encrypted data storage
* Row-level security ensuring users can access only their own data

Supabase is GDPR-compliant and follows modern security standards.
Read their privacy information here: info@softcomputers.ca

FocusFlow does **not** integrate with Spotify or other third-party services.

5. Notifications

FocusFlow may send **local notifications** on your device for:

* Session completion
* Daily reminders
* Habit reminders

Notification data is **not transmitted** to our servers or any third parties.

6. Children’s Privacy

FocusFlow is not intended for children under 4.
We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

If you believe a child has provided us with data, contact us, and we will delete it promptly.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data only while your account is active.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at:

info@softcomputers.ca

Upon deletion:

* Your account is permanently removed
* All synced focus sessions, habits, and stats are erased
* All profile information is deleted

Local device data may remain until the app is deleted.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have the right to:

* Access your personal data
* Correct inaccurate information
* Request deletion
* Request a copy of your data
* Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise these rights, email us at info@softcomputers.ca
9. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices, including:

* HTTPS encryption
* Secure password handling (via Supabase)
* Row-level security policies
* Strict access controls

While no system is perfectly secure, we continually update our practices to protect your information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the App evolves.
If significant changes occur, we will notify users within the App.


11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact:

Email: info@softcomputers.ca
